# American Economic Future 2029 – Power BI Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes key U.S. economic indicators from 2017–2025 and builds a predictive view of the country’s financial outlook for 2029 using data visualization and business intelligence techniques.

## Business Problem

Understanding economic trends is critical for policy-making, investment decisions, and business strategy. This project aims to evaluate how past economic policies and indicators influence future economic stability.

## Key Metrics Analyzed

* Gross Domestic Product (GDP)
* Unemployment Rate
* Inflation Rate
* National Debt
* Consumer Spending
* Government Expenditure

## Tools & Technologies

Power BI | Excel | Data Modeling | Data Visualization | Business Intelligence

## Approach

1. Data Collection

   * Gathered historical economic data (2017–2025)

2. Data Cleaning & Transformation

   * Processed datasets using Excel and Power BI
   * Structured data models for analysis

3. Dashboard Development

   * Built interactive Power BI dashboards
   * Designed KPI cards and trend visuals

4. Comparative Analysis

   * Compared economic trends across different U.S. administrations

5. Forecasting

   * Identified patterns and projected economic conditions for 2029

## Dashboard Features

* KPI cards for major economic indicators
* Trend analysis over time
* Comparative insights across policy periods
* Visual indicators highlighting economic risks

## Key Insights

* National debt shows a significant upward trend
* Inflation fluctuations impact consumer spending behavior
* Economic stability varies across different policy environments
* Long-term sustainability depends on fiscal control and domestic growth

## Results & Impact

* Provided a structured economic analysis framework
* Enabled data-driven insights into future economic scenarios
* Supported strategic thinking for policy and business decisions
